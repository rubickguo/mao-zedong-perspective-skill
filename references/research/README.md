# Research Index

This folder contains the research notes behind the `mao-zedong-perspective` skill.

The notes are not meant to be a complete biography or a final historical judgment. They are working notes for building a perspective skill: what reasoning patterns to preserve, what period differences to respect, and what safety risks to keep visible.

## How To Use The Notes

- Use the research notes to check whether a response is grounded in a known method, text, period, or external evaluation.
- Do not use the notes to invent quotations. If a quoted source is needed, verify it against the linked primary or secondary source.
- For modern topics, treat outputs as analogical extrapolation from the method, not as Mao's historical view of the modern case.
- For unsafe requests, use the notes to explain historical risk, coercive failure modes, and ethical boundaries rather than to provide operational guidance.

## Mode-To-Research Mapping

| Skill Mode | Start With | Why |
|------------|------------|-----|
| Method lens | `01-writings.md`, then `05-decisions.md` | Keeps modern analysis anchored in method rather than invented historical claims |
| Historical explanation | `06-timeline.md`, then the relevant source file | Forces period and context before interpretation |
| Voice experiment | `03-expression-dna.md`, then `01-writings.md` | Preserves recognizable expression while keeping the analytical skeleton visible |
| Safety downgrade | `04-external-views.md`, then `05-decisions.md` | Keeps coercive politics, over-mobilization, and movement-style failure risks explicit |

## Source Honesty Checklist

Before using these notes in an answer, check:

- Is the claim a source-backed fact, an interpretation, or a modern analogy?
- Does the answer identify the period being discussed when period matters?
- Is any quotation directly verifiable from a linked source?
- Does the answer preserve both analytical value and destructive historical risk?
- Does the answer avoid turning disagreement, workplace conflict, or ordinary product strategy into enemy politics?

## 中文说明

这个目录是 `mao-zedong-perspective` 的研究底稿索引，不是完整传记，也不是最终历史评价。它的用途是帮助维护者判断：某个回答应该依据哪类材料、属于哪种模式、需要保留哪些安全边界。

使用时注意：

- **方法论分析**：优先看 `01-writings.md` 和 `05-decisions.md`，把现代问题标成类比推演。
- **历史解释**：优先看 `06-timeline.md`，再查对应文本或决策材料，避免混淆早中晚期。
- **表达实验**：优先看 `03-expression-dna.md`，但不能用表层口吻替代分析。
- **安全降级**：优先看 `04-external-views.md` 和 `05-decisions.md`，突出过度动员、强制政治和运动化治理的风险。

凡是涉及“原话”“出处”“他当时怎么说”，必须回到可核验来源；找不到就明确说找不到，不要代写成引号。
